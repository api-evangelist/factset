# FactSet

> FactSet creates flexible, open data and software solutions for tens of thousands of investment professionals around the world, providing instant access to financial data and analytics that investors use to make crucial decisions. The FactSet developer portal catalogs 100+ REST APIs spanning prices, fundamentals, estimates, entity/symbology, portfolio analytics, quantitative engines, ESG, news, documents, and reporting, all served from https://api.factset.com with OAuth 2.0 (auth.factset.com) or API-key authentication and an Enterprise SDK in Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Java, and .NET.

Generated by API Evangelist from the factset apis.yml catalog and repo artifacts (no provider-published llms.txt was found at developer.factset.com/llms.txt or www.factset.com/llms.txt as of 2026-07-22).
